Where to Stay

Important: Book by the end of February! The wedding weekend is during the main tourism season, and there is a jazz festival in the city that weekend. We pre-selected three hotels and hostels close that are located near to the shuttle drop off and pick up point (Dachauplatz). We picked three price ranges:

Hotel Luis
  • Double room is ~89€.
  • Important: Book via email at rezeption@hotel-luis.de, and tell them you're with the Alex and Frido party)
  • On the edge of the historic city center, an eleven minute walk from the train station, a nine minute walk from the shuttle pick up and drop off
  • NO air conditioning
Hotel VIII
  • Price for a double room is ~130€.
  • Very close to the Danube, four minute walk to the shuttle location, fifteen minute walk from the train station
Microverse Hostel/Apartments
  • Walk about 15 minutes from the shuttle that drops people off after the wedding. But it's a nice walk through the historic city.
  • They have the standard dorm beds and private rooms, however the best option is to book one of their appartments. These appartments are for a group of people (2 to 6) ranging in per person/per night price from 35€ to 50€.
  • We recommend that you inquire with them what size appartments they still have and then organize a group of friends.
