Where to Stay

Important: Book by the end of February or March! The wedding weekend is during the main tourism season, and there is a jazz festival in the city that weekend. We pre-selected three hotels and hostels close that are located near to the shuttle drop off and pick up point (Dachauplatz). Here an overview of where things are in Regensburg. Click on the markers for more information.

Hotel Luis
  • Price: Double: 89€.
  • How to book: Contact the hotel directly at rezeption@hotel-luis.de under "Hochzeit Alex & Frido". Rooms are blocked until March 23th 2018. You can cancel your researvation for free up to 24 hours before the stay.
  • Location: On the edge of the historic city center, 10 minute walk from the train station, a nine minute walk from the shuttle pick up and drop off
  • NO air conditioning
Hotel VIII
  • How to book: Contact the hotel directly at info@hotel8-regensburg.de under "Hochzeit Alex & Frido". Rooms are blocked until March 31th 2018.
  • Price: Single: 130€ ($150); Double: 140€ ($170).
  • Location:Very close to the Danube, four minute walk to the shuttle location, fifteen minute walk from the train station.
Star Inn Hotel
  • How to book: Contact the hotel directly at regensburg.zentrum@starinnhotels.com under "Hochzeit Alex & Frido". Rooms are blocked until June 6th 2018.
  • Price: Single: 79€ ($98); Double: 89€ ($110); Family (2 adults + 1-2 children under 13 years): 99€ ($120); Family (3 person): 109€ ($135); Family (4 person): 119€ ($150). Breakfast is an additional 14€. Prices are reduced for 6/19 and 6/20.
  • Location:Next to the main train station (close to the shuttle on the day of the wedding). 5 minutes walk to the historic city center.
