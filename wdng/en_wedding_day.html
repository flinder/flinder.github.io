Program

All times are approximate.
  • 1:00 - 1:45pm - Guest Arrival
  • 2:00pm - Wedding ceremony
  • 2:45pm - Reception
  • 4:00pm - Coffee and Pie
  • 6:30pm - Cocktail Hour
  • 7:30pm - Dinner
  • Open end

Getting to the wedding

A shuttle to the wedding leaves from Burgweinting and from the city center at 1pm:
  • Burgweinting: Leaves 1.00pm in front of the Hotel Gasthof Parzefall
  • City center: Leaves 1.00pm in front of the Historical Museum (at the bus stop).

Getting home

There will be a shuttle going to the city center (drop-off at the Historical Museum) and to Burgweinting (drop-off at Gasthof Parzefall) starting at 11:30pm approximately every 40 - 60 minutes.
